# my page

brief - 
develop a digital solution for Greenfield Local Hub (GLH)
A co-op of local farmers and food producers. they offer
- a single location retail outlet for locally produced food and drink
- collective marketing for local farmers and food producers
- product management, stock control and product traceability

client wants to develop digital solution that will
- provide info about producers, their methods and benefits of buying locally
- allow customers to view products with transparent pricing and availability, place collection or delivery orders, manage accounts and view order history
- provide dashboard to enable producers to manage stock levels, update product details and view orders

suggested features:
- order management, scheduling and tracking
- loyalty scheme to provide discounts and special offers
- accessibility features to support a wide range of users
